    PROFILE

    Kelly Kong is originally from China, and is a New York-based Creative Technologist. She is currently studying in Parsons School of Design majoring in Design and Technology in her third year. Most of her projects focuses on immersive experiences that balances playfulness and practicality. She has been working on personal projects focused on Augmented and Virtual Reality with her peer on a startup which is called "EGG" — experience generated gear.

    Her goal is to incorporate creative technology to assist in different fields of design, especially in areas of design that encompasses spatiality hoping to elucidate them. As for her other interests, she takes pleasure in exploring spaces that fascinate her, hanging out with cats, and finding sentient moments from her surroundings.
