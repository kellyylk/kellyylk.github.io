    • BIOSPHERE OF TWO DRAGONFLIES

    (2018)

    This is a dragonfly installation to play with the connection between the organic and the artificial. The purpose of making this was to present the insect’s biotic form and habitat in an artificial manner to see what potential outcome I would get. My motive was to experiment while I was creating, seeking further inspiration. The animation project represents day, dawn, and life in a synthetic way by how the form, color, and movement of every frame is rendered.
